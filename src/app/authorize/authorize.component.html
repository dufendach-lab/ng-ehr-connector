How it works

Our mission is to provide the best care for our patients. By connecting your Electronic Health Record, we are able to collect valuable information to help us better understand your pregnancy.

After selecting your hospital from the list above, you will be redirected to your hospitals MyChart where you will sign in and connect your EHR. Once there, you will see what information is accessible.

After allowing access, you will be redirected back to our site to view this information. From here you will be able to select what to send to Cincinnati Children's.
